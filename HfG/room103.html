Welcome to Room 103

Question: Do you have ideas that you would like to develop?
Answer: Yes.

Q: What are the areas of interest?
A: I get excited about a lot of things very fast. But my main interest (of course) is how new technologies inbed themselves in society and change human behaviour. In the past I had several research topics including Buddhism, Superorganisms like Ants, Attention Economy, Rating Systems, Big Data, Machine Learning, Public Art, Conceptual Art and Activism, Russian Trolls [...] Q: What would you like to investigate?

A: There are several things I am investigating at the moment, one of which, as stated before, is how rating systems are implemented online, but also found themselves into the analog world. One example I thought was interesting was at the airport in Vienna after the security check.

Q: Where do you position yourself at this moment as future author?
A: I consider myself to be a part of a group of artists who try to directly comment on the internet at large. As the internet is ubiquitous nowadays some of them are called "Post-Internet-Artists". Maybe I am late stage post-internet. Maybe we shoul invent a new term...

Q: At which interest areas, mediums and apart from your private projects are you interested making part of collective works?
A: I am always happy to help other artists with interesting projects that involve programming. Working for an with others helps me to find interesting tasks and creative solutions. Also I am very happy to create and curate exhibitions and their promotional material.

Q: Why did you choose this University? A: I don't know. It's kind of a media art institution, right?

Q: What do you expect from the University?
A: Basically I just want to find new friends, have fun and develop my artistic career.

Q: What do you expect from the teacher?
A: Some input would be cool every now and then, but I see my teachers more as friends and colleagues, so I hope to discuss art on eye level.

Q: Do you want to be part of the Moving Images Students?
A: I guess...

Q: Which authors/artists are you interested in?
A: Aram Bartholl, Jan Robert Leegte, Constant Dullart, Jeremy Bailey, Anthony Antonellis, Dries Depoorter, Angela Washko, Egor Kraft and many many more...
